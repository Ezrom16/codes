About Me

I am an Environmental Health Officer with a degree from Muhimbili University of Health and Allied Sciences (MUHAS). My academic background and field experience have equipped me with strong skills in environmental risk assesment, sanitation, food safety, water quality monitoring, and disease prevention. I am committed to promoting public health through practical, science-based solutions that create healthier and safer communities.

Through this platform, I aim to share knowledge, raise awareness, and engage with individuals and organisations working toward sustainable environmental health practices. Whether you're here to learn, collaborate, or seek professional insight, I welcome you to explore and connect.
