Published on: May 25, 2025

In May 2025, our environmental health team was alerted to a sudden increase in diarrhea cases in a rural community. After a quick assessment, we identified a contaminated water source used by over 500 residents.

Our immediate steps included water sampling, community education, and providing chlorine-based water purification tablets. We also partnered with local leaders to isolate the source and distribute clean water alternatives.

Within just 5 days, we saw a dramatic drop in new cases. This event highlighted the importance of fast, coordinated response and community collaboration in controlling outbreaks.
